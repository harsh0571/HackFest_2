Beach Bliss

Goa Beaches

Discover the vibrant and lively beaches of Goa, perfect for parties and water sports.

Baga Beach

Known for its vibrant nightlife and water sports, Baga Beach is one of Goa's most popular destinations.

  • Water sports available: Parasailing, jet skiing, banana boat rides
  • Famous for its nightlife and beach shacks
  • Best time to visit: November to February

Calangute Beach

Often called the 'Queen of Beaches', Calangute offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern amenities.

  • Longest beach in North Goa
  • Great for swimming and sunbathing
  • Numerous restaurants and shopping options nearby
